YourApps - Large Partner Program

What is it?

We know a few very large servers are currently using yourapps for day-to-day applications!

As a token of our gratitude, servers with more than 10,000 members are eligible to free YourApps premium!

How do I redeem?

Make sure you meet all of the following requirements:

  • Your server has 10,000 or more server members (excluding bots)
  • You have one or more open position in use
  • You have at least 2 open positions people can apply for
  • You're an admin or above in the server

If you meet all of those requirements, please join our Support Server, open a ticket via t!support and ask for the large partner program.
